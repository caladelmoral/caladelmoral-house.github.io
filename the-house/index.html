The house in detail

Living room and terrace

The living room has 2 comfortable sofas with capacity for 5-6 people where you can watch the TV or simply relax. You can access the terrace from the living room, where you can have breakfast with the morning sun.

Kitchen

The kitchen is full equipped with an electric Hob, fridge freezer, cook, oven, microwaves and dishwasher besides dishware, pots and pans. There’s also a washing machine.

Bedrooms

There are 3 bedrooms with wardrobes.
